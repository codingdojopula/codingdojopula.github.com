Coding Dojo has come to Pula / Croatia :-) The concept is nothing new, so no use spending time there. We meet usually every first (or second) Saturday at Hacklab space (a branch of Monte Paradiso club) in Rojc. All wandering developers are welcome, feel free to drop in! You can join us @ our Google group or look into Twitter's #codingdojopula tag.
